# UNICO — Brand Specification

## Overview
UNICO is a Nepali clothing brand for young adults — casual wear with a warm, modern minimal aesthetic.

## Typography
- **Display**: 'DM Serif Display', 'Playfair Display', Georgia, serif
- **Body**: 'Inter', -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', system-ui, sans-serif
- **Mono**: 'JetBrains Mono', 'IBM Plex Mono', ui-monospace, monospace

## Posture
- Warm neutral canvas (Alabaster Glow), not cool grey
- Serif display for brand moments, sans body for readability
- Generous whitespace, clean grid, one accent (Toasted Pecan) used sparingly
- Rounded corners (8–12px), soft shadows, no heavy borders
