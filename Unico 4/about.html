Born in Kathmandu,
worn everywhere.

UNICO is a Nepali clothing brand built for the everyday. We make clothes that move with you — from the narrow streets of Patan to anywhere your day takes you.

UNICO started in a small studio in Lalitpur with a simple idea: that the clothes we wear every day deserve the same care as the moments they carry us through. No fast fashion. No disposable trends. Just well-made pieces designed to live in.

"We work directly with local makers — the same families who have been weaving and stitching in the Kathmandu Valley for generations."

Every UNICO garment is cut and sewn in partnership with Nepali workshops. We use premium cotton and low-impact dyes. The fit is modern — clean lines, considered proportions — but the soul is pure Nepal: patient, unhurried, made to last.

We launched in 2024 with a single hoodie. Today we make hoodies, tees, and bottoms in small batches — enough to keep quality high and waste low. Each drop tells a piece of the story we're still writing.

What we stand for

✎

Handcrafted

Every piece is made by hand in Kathmand Valley workshops. No assembly lines — just skilled makers who take pride in their work.

♻

Sustainably Minded

Small-batch production, low-impact dyes, minimal packaging. We make less so each piece matters more.

★

Built to Last

Double-stitched seams, premium cotton, hardware that holds. We design for years, not seasons.

Made in Nepal

From fabric sourcing to final stitch, our supply chain stays within 50 km of Kathmandu. It's better for the planet, better for our community, and better for the people who wear UNICO.
